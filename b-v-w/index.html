Bears vs wolves

Who wins a battle between a full grown bear and a wolfpack?

Let’s set the stage. Both parties are very hungry and desperate for food. The bear is full-sized adult in its prime, and the pack has 7 wolves. They find the abandoned remains of a freshly shot and killed moose.

There’s no time limit, and neither party will back down until the other is dead. There must be a winner! Who would you pick to win the contest?

Pros for the Bear

  • Can easily handle at least 2 wolves on its own
  • Superior strength and stamina
  • Can camp at the site of the carcass
  • Can maul or bite a wolf to quickly disable it

Pros for the Wolf pack

  • Strength in numbers. If they all attack at once
  • Can lure bear away from carcass and use guerilla warfare tactics
